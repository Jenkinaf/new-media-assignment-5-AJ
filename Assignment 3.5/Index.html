All About Waffles

They're the biz

The majestic waffle, a delicious slab of griddled scrumptiousness, carbs and sugar slathered in more sugar. Ugh, so good. Make it healthy with some berries! That fixes everything.

Waffles are undisputed as the tastiest breakfast bread-like, according to all four people I pretended to ask about them. They soundly defeat the pancake and narrowly rebuff the biscuit.
